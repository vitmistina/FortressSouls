# Research Spike

## Start

1. Read `AGENTS.md`, the spike question, and relevant research/runbooks.
2. Define the decision, evidence needed, limits, and stop conditions.
3. Inspect existing artifacts before creating a probe.

## Validate

Use the smallest reproducible, read-only experiment. Preserve commands and sanitized evidence; separate observed facts from inference.

## Summary

- Question and recommendation
- Evidence and reproduction steps
- Risks and unknowns
- Files changed
- Next decision or backlog item
